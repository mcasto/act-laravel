Where to Find ACT

We are located in our own theater in El Centro, Cuenca at Antonio Vega Muñoz 14-46, between Coronel Tálbot and Estévez de Toral. (View on Google Maps) . We are near the Coronel Tálbot Tranvía stop, with frequent bus service along Antonio Vega Muñoz. It's a short walk from Parque Calderón (see map below) and just 8 minutes on foot from Parque San Sebastián.

Route from Parque Calderón to ACT
tram
Tranvía (Eastbound)
Coming from Gringolandia? Exit at Coronel Tálbot stop, walk west less than a block, turn right on Coronel Tálbot, walk three blocks north, then right on Vega Muñoz. Look for the Afrodita sign — and someone will be outside to welcome you.
tram
Tranvía (Westbound)
Coming from the airport? Exit at Mercado 3 de Noviembre (idiomART stop), walk west less than a block, turn right on Coronel Tálbot, two blocks north, then right on Vega Muñoz. Same Afrodita sign — and a friendly ACT volunteer out front.
local_taxi
Taxi
Give the driver: Vega Muñoz 14-46, between Estévez de Toral and Coronel Tálbot. Walk half a block west from Estévez de Toral (or east from Coronel Tálbot). Look for the Afrodita sign.
directions_bus
Bus
Many buses stop nearby. Use the Moovit app for best routes — or contact us with your bus line, and we'll help.
directions_car
Driving
Vega Muñoz runs east-west (one-way). From Estévez de Toral (northbound), turn left onto Vega Muñoz. We're halfway down the block on the right (north side). Look for the Afrodita sign. Limited parking is available behind the theater — watch for the open driveway.
