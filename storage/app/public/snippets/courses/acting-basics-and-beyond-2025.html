So, you want to act?

But you wonder if you have what it takes (or if you still have it). Good news. You do.

Acting is the art of being human—fully and completely. It's nothing more than that. Emotional honesty communicated between the members of a cast and to an audience.

You've been "humaning" for a while, right? Feeling feelings? Emoting emotions? Then you've got what you need. Emotions, a body, a bit of courage (which is a learned skill, not something you're "born with"), and you're ready to start.

The rest is just skills. I can teach you those. It's not easy, but it's definitely doable. In eight lessos. Two hours each. Dress to move. Be ready to sweat a bit, maybe cry a bit. Think quite a bit.

Bring a pen and a notebook. You're going to want to write some of this down.
